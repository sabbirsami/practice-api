1. Discuss the scope of var, let, and const

Let, Const & Var are used to declare variable. The scope of var variable is functional scope. It can be re-assign & updated. Var variable can be declared without initialization. On the other hand The scope of let variable is block scope. you can updated let variable but you cannot re-declare it. And The scope of Const variable is also block scope like let. it's almost smeller with let variable but you can't updated const variable & you cannot re-declare it.
